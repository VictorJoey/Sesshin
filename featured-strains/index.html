Dessert Overview

Dolato, also known as "Do-Si-Lato" and "Dosi-Lato," is an indica marijuana strain made by crossing Do-Si-Dos with Gelato #41. Dolato’s terpene profile features a fruity, earthy scent followed by a sweet lavender taste.

Dough Lato Overview

Dough Lato is a hybrid strain with the lineage of Gelato 41, Dosido, and Moonbow. This strain has an aroma to match its beauty—a sweet, gassy-gelato smell, much like its Gelato parent. Known as a focusing strain that is popular for a slowed down night in, perhaps paired with a drawn bath and a book.

Gelato Overview

Also known as Gelato 33 or Larry Bird, Gelato is an Indica-dominant cross of Sunset Sherbet x Thin Mint Girl Scout Cookies, bred by Sherbinskis in San Francisco. This delicious cultivar has a sweet, fruity flavor with a creamy finish. Gelato provides relaxing effects accompanied by euphoric high.

Sour Diesel Overview

Sour Diesel is a Sativa-leaning hybrid cannabis consisting of 90% Sativa. This diesel weed is an offspring of Chemdawg and Super Skunk. You can see where it inherited its potency and diesel-like smell. Its THC level is about 19-25% but can go up to 30%.
